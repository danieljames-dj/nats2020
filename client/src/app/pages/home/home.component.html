Cancellation of Indian Nationals 2020
In light of the current situation regarding COVID-19 both in India as well as around the world, it is with great regret that we inform you that Indian Nationals 2020 stands CANCELLED.

All fee paid towards Competition registration, Accommodation, and Merchandise will be refunded 100% EXCLUDING the Payment Gateway charges and refunds will be processed shortly.

We would like to thank the WCA Delegates, Sponsors, and the cubing community for supporting us this far.

Until next time!

The Legacy
Speedcubers from around the country come together for an intense puzzle-solving competition, every year - the Indian Nationals. With every passing year, the event has lived up to the expections of being exciting for not only the participants but also the viewers.
The city of Chennai is all geared up to welcome competitors and guests from all around for the Indian Nationals 2020.

Key information

Dates: April 23-26, 2020
Location: Chengalpattu, Tamil Nadu
Venue: SRM Institute of Science and Technology
The venue
Chennai is the capital city of the state with enormous cultural importance, Tamil Nadu. Also known as the Cultural Capital of India, the city has far more to offer than what meets the eye.
The competition will be held at SRM Institute of Science and Technology's TP Ganesan Auditorium, which is one of Asia's Largest Pillarless Auditoriums. Chennai is proud to be hosting the Indian Nationals 2020.
